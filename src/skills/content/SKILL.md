# CONTENT CREATION SKILL

## Purpose
Generate high-quality written content for marketing, products, and communication.

## Status
Advanced

## Capabilities
- Write blog posts and articles
- Create social media content
- Draft email campaigns
- Write product descriptions
- Generate sales copy
- Create educational content

## Frameworks
### AIDA (Attention, Interest, Desire, Action)
```
Attention: Hook the reader
Interest: Build curiosity
Desire: Show benefits
Action: Call to action
```

### PAS (Problem, Agitate, Solution)
```
Problem: Identify pain point
Agitate: Make it worse
Solution: Present your product
```

### Storytelling
```
Setup → Conflict → Resolution → Moral
```

## Content Types

### Social Media
- Twitter threads: 5-10 tweets, hook first
- LinkedIn posts: Professional, value-first
- Instagram: Visual-focused, short caption

### Email
- Subject lines: Under 50 chars, curiosity
- Body: Personal tone, scannable
- CTA: Clear, single action

### Long-form
- Structure: H1, H2, bullet points
- Length: 1000-3000 words
- SEO: Natural keyword usage

## Best Practices
1. Know your audience
2. Lead with value
3. Use active voice
4. Keep it scannable
5. Include clear CTAs
6. Proofread before publishing

## Templates

### Product Launch Email
```
Subject: [Big benefit] + [Urgency]

Hi [Name],

[Hook - surprising stat or bold claim]

[Problem - what they struggle with]

[Solution - your product]

[Proof - testimonial or result]

[Offer - what they get + price]

[Urgency - limited time/offer]

[CTA button]

[Signature]
```

### Twitter Thread
```
Tweet 1 (Hook):
The [X] mistake that costs [audience] $[amount]/year

Tweets 2-8 (Value):
[Point 1 with insight]
[Point 2 with example]
[Point 3 with tip]
...

Tweet 9 (CTA):
Follow for more [topic] tips

Tweet 10 (Engagement):
What's your biggest [pain point]? Reply below 👇
```
